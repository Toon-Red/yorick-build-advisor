Who is Yorick Mori? 
 
Yorick Mori in short words is a member of the Brethren of The Dusk, an order of 
Monks from the Blessed Isles, Yorick is a Gravedigger and in league of Legends 
a Necromancer & Pet based Champion, Blessed and cursed with the power to 
See and Hear the Dead.. 
 
He's a Toplaner Juggernaut, which is a group of mostly immobile but strong 
champions that are a mix of Fighters & Tank, Such as Aatrox, Mundo and 
Mordekaiser.. 
 
His playstyle revolves around Splitpushing and putting a lot of pressure on the 
map and enemies with his horde of pets by his side, which all come with a lot of 
pros and cons.. 
Pros & Cons 
 
Pros: 
- "Simple Kit" / Beginner Friendly 
- Strong Pets 
- Tilts The enemy  
- Able to Push 3 lanes at once 
- Insane Macro Tools to stall & End Games 
 
Cons: 
- Slow & Unreliable / Low CC 
- No "Real" Control over his pets 
- Bugs, Bad AI, Team will hate you.. 
- Hard to carry when all lanes lost hard 
- No Defensive Tools / "Squishy Tank"

R- Eulogy Of The Isles: 
Yorick Summons the Maiden of the Mist that deals ranged Magic DMG and %HP 
Magic DMG when Yorick attacks the same champion he is attacking, while 
converting nearby enemy unit deaths into Ghouls. 
 
Yorick's Abilities on paper look very simple, which makes him beginner friendly.. 
He Throws Pocket Sand, makes his pets Aggro you, then Traps you followed by 
a Bonk as his Pets maul you since.. 
 
He is a pet champion which is known to annoy and tilt the enemy similar to 
Heimer & Zyra, though his pets are mobile unlike theirs and can do a lot more.. 
This gives Yorick a unique macro decisions that other champions do not think 
about, this gives him insane Macro tools and gives him the power to manipulate 
waves, "shadow push" (more on this later on) 
 
Though he's very slow as a Juggernaut, He's not very tanky by default, he's only 
situationally Tanky with his Q, but cannot keep himself alive since he lacks 
defensive tools. 
His biggest con is how many bugs his kit is riddled with and the fact he cannot 
really control his pets or have readily access to them which makes it hard for him 
to carry if everyone is lost, since splitpush is more effective when you're team is 
going even or is winning, otherwise it's more of a stalling tool.

P - Shepherd of Soul 
Yorick's Passive grants him a passive grave every 8th-2nd enemy unit that dies 
around him, His Q Last Rites can also grant him graves if he Kills with it or Hits 
an enemy Champion or Large Monster. 
 
The passive itself is mostly his Pets, The Mist Walkers or how I call them, The 
Ghouls, these are his minions that fight for him, he can only have 4 of them 
around himself and his R, The Maiden of The Mist. 
 
Once Yorick has 2-4 Graves around him, after using his Q he can re-cast it to 
summon 2-4 Ghouls from the graves, as long as the graves are within his circular 
range, 
 
Seems pretty straight forward but there's many tips and tricks around the rules of 
him and his Maiden only having 4 Ghouls, you can even Alt+Click his passive to 
check how many ghouls are near you or around the whole map.. 
 
Grave Tips 
While recasting Yorick's Q Awakening, you can walk away from a grave while 
having 4 graves, to only summon 3 or even 1 Grave, because the recast will only 
summon Ghouls from the graves within the Circle range of the Recast. 
 
Seems pretty useless but there's also an invisible circle around Yorick that's 
slightly bigger (1000 units around Yorick), this one is the size of almost the entire 
screen, this one tracks how many Ghouls are around Yorick and you can see this 
by his passive.

The Mist Walkers - Ghouls  
Yorick cannot control his ghouls fully, but if you understand their AI you can make 
good use of them. Before we get into their AI and how they react, let's list some 
Facts & Stats about them. 
 
Ghouls Facts & Stats: 
●​ - They are dumb, but lvl up alongside Yorick, scaling up their stats. 
●​ - 325 base move speed, but gain an extra 60 when near Yorick and any 
other FLAT MS Yorick gains, Phase rush is the only %MS they benefit 
from. 
●​ - Ghouls lose their 60 bonus move speed when they're far away from 
Yorick but when they still tethered to him they will gain bonus MS based on 
how far away they are from him, making them run very fast.. 
●​ - 110-300 Scaling HP with 15% Bonus HP Ratio and 15-100 AD scaling 
with 20% Bonus AD. 
●​ - 125 attack range and 0.54-0.9 attack speed. 
●​ - Ghouls do not count as "minions" so they won't lower a tower's defense 
and let you deal full DMG to them like lane minions do. 
●​ - Their auto attacks are affected by Blinds (Teemo & Quinn), Dodges (Jax 
& Nilah) and Blocks (Shen) 
●​ - They're autos (Basic attacks) deal Area of Effect DMG (AoE), but when 
it's during their leap attack against a marked target of Yorick's E, it counts 
as an basic attack. 
●​ - They're Basic attacks, can apply Spell effects, but not On-hit effects, this 
is why they can apply Liandry & Comet but not Titanic Hydra or Blade of 
The Ruined King.

Ghouls AI Training: 
- Ghouls when spawned will stand behind Yorick (200 units) untill they enter 
"combat" 
 
- Ghouls enter "Combat" when they are nearby an enemy Minions wave or 
enemy champion, after this they will NOT follow you anymore and will proceed in 
that lane. 
 
- Ghouls can exist Combat if they are aggro'd unto an enemy Monster with your 
E, or champion, after they kill the monster or champion or lose vision of either of 
them, they will follow Yorick again. 
 
- If you're recalling they will follow you home, unless they get in combat with 
minions or monsters, or champions, but hextech gate will force them to TP to you 
if close enough. 
(They do not follow you IN or OUT OF Mordekasier's Ult though..) 
 
- Against Multi camp monsters like Krugs, Raptors or wolves, Ghouls will only 
aggro ONE unit even if they attack multiple ones, so once the E mark is gone or 
their target die they will go back to yorick. 
(So you can E the Big Raptor chicken, they will kill 1 then leave, same with 
wolves or if they kill the big krug, they will leave the small ones) 
 
- When a Target enters a bush the ghouls will de-aggro and stop attacking them, 
same if they lose vision of them, this can even occur when the E mark is still 
active (bug). 
 
- They're dumb and very buggy, I'll go over the known "good" and "bad" bugs in 
the Bugs section, they will drop aggro randomly very often when chasing and 
cancel auto attacks, so beware..

Ghouls Tips & Tricks 
Passive Income 
Ghouls & Maiden give 100% Gold & Experience when they last hit minions, this 
also counts towards them assisting hitting a tower, if a plate goes down 5-10s~ 
later you will gain full Gold from it as well. 
Artificial Minions 
Ghouls & Maiden do not count as minions, so if you go to hit a tower with them 
without LANE MINIONS, the tower will have full defense. 
 
But if the tower is low and you have Grubs, Demolish or Hullbreaker you can 
force a tower by letting your Wall tank it alongside your pGhouls. 
 
You can also raise your pets in lane when an enemy is pushing you in or when 
you don't have a chance to land E and the wave is coming to your tower, the 
minions will aggro your pets, thus stopping the siege of the enemy minions, you 
can use this to stall the push till your real lane minions show up, which can result 
in a freeze. 
Shadow Push 
Because Ghouls & Maiden give 100% Gold & EXP, you can leave them in lanes 
before you recall (After lane phase) to rack up EXP & Gold. 
Since Gold & EXP ramps up mid to late game this becomes a very powerful tool 
to catch up or snowball your Gold & LVL advantage. 
Face Check Ghouls 
Ghouls can give full vision in bushes, so if you can E Rift, Baron, Grubs over wall 
or an enemy they will grant you vision.

her or Ghouls are off doing something else you don't share them anymore, so 
you can have more than 4 on the map.

Q - Last Rites  
AA Reset & Range 
Yorick’s Q is an enhanced basic attack,this ability can reset your auto attack, 
meaning it resets it’s timer and you’re not confined by your attack speed or 
animation time, giving Yorick a small combo of AA (Auto attack) + Q Bonk. 
 
Like most enhanced auto attacks, Yorick’s Q grants his next auto (Q) more 
range, in his case it gives 50 bonus attack range, this can be used to spam out 
enemies or farm from a slightly safe distance despite being a melee champion. 
 
Because Yorick has a built in auto attack reset, he can attack twice quickly, which 
can then proc his R’s %HP twice as well, because The Maiden of The Mist. 
​
The auto attack reset will also help in Season 16 now that Demolish requires 3 
auto attacks, thus giving Yorick an advantage to AA + Q + AA again for 3 hits and 
proc the Demolish and acquire tower plating gold. 
Graves 
Of course our Q is our real grave passive, which guarantees us graves when Q: 
●​ Last Hit and Kill with Q 
●​ Q an enemy Champion 
●​ Q “Large” enemy monsters (Big Wolf, Raptor, Krug, Gromp & Scuttle) 
●​ Q Epic Monsters ( Grubs, Rift Herald, Dragon, Atakhan & Baron) 
 
Hullbreaker is another item that Yorick as a splitpusher will want and also 
requires a certain amount of autos to proc it, in this case 5, which is a lot..

W - Dark Procession 
Dark Procession is Yorick’s Wall, this wall is not instant and raises itself after 
0.75s, once it’s coming up it’s edges can knock up enemies and displace them 
giving a minor CC for 0.25s. 
 
Within 210 units of it you are pushed inside of the wall, otherwise the wall will 
push you away, this wall can only be targeted by champions basic attacks or 
tower shots, Champs deal 1 HP to it while towers will one shot the wall. 
 
Inside the wall you are given sight, meaning you can spawn the wall over another 
wall to check vision, though niche it can be useful to know this.. 
Yorick’s wall will remain up for 4 seconds unless it is destroyed, its cooldown also 
starts upon cast and does not stop Yorick’s movement (walking) like his E does.. 
This means while running away you can summon the wall and keep running, 
while if you throw E it will stop Yorick from walking, thus slowing you down.

3W Max Order 
Because of this and the fact the wall is big and its HP can scale up to 4 HP, 
starting at 2 HP, it is very important to always put at least 3 points into Yorick’s

reset, so it’s easy for them to Auto and use their enhanced auto to instantly break 
the wall, but with 3 HP the wall will remain at 1 HP buying you and your pets time 
to deal more DMG, this is super effective and a MUST against champions with no

move, then auto untill they enemy or they get out of range, which is not an 
efficient way of attacking somebody..

repeat this pattern to increase their DPS, otherwise the enemy will walk out of 
range and you will either cancel auto attack due to not being in range anymore or 
waste time walking into range, but if you’re fast enough your DPS will sky rocket, 
this is why Master Yi’s R giving him movement speed is strong. 
 
This is the real reason why Serylda’s Grudge was strong on Yorick back when it

the enemy to be below half HP to apply the slow.
