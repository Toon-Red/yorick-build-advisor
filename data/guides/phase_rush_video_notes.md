# Phase Rush Yorick Tech — Video Notes
Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HXOKwLxDRIU (Kampsycho, Masters climb)

## Key Points

### Phase Rush Rune Setup
- **Primary**: Phase Rush + Manaflow Band + Celerity + Scorch
  - Celerity buffs movement speed from Phase Rush even more
  - Manaflow Band by default (Nimbus Cloak if fine on mana)
  - Transcendence alternative if not chasing hard
- **Secondary**: Resolve — Demolish + [Bone Plating / Second Wind / Revitalize]
  - Bone Plating = usually the default
  - Second Wind = if fighting ranged
  - Revitalize = if can go sustain
- **Shards**: AS + Movement Speed + Tenacity
  - Celerity + MS shard = base 352 MS (same as Trundle, Nasus, Jax, Tryndamere)
  - Counters can't chase you anymore

### When to Take Phase Rush
- Main reason for Masters climb — "most of it was done on phase rush"
- NOT all matchups — "some matchups still remain the same"
- Best when: enemy team has lots of people you need to chase or run away from
- Specifically mentioned matchups:
  - **Gwen**: Phase Rush gives slow resistance vs her R/Rylai's. "Otherwise I would have to just ban Gwen"
  - **Jax**: Wall him as he jumps, proc Phase Rush, kite when counter strike is up, fight when it's down
  - **Mordekaiser**: Exhaust instead of Ghost ("I got exhaust except instead of my ghost")
  - **Ash (Ashe) top**: "I would just go airy and bramble into boots"
  - Champions with built-in slows (Ashe support example)

### Phase Rush Mechanics
- Only takes a few auto attacks from ghouls + Maiden to proc it
- Gives enough MS to chase, run away, or create opportunities
- Slow resistance helps vs Gwen R, Rylai's users
- Q heals more the lower HP you are — use with Phase Rush to weave in/out while waiting for Q cooldown
- Diving with Phase Rush is "way easier since you can run in and out of danger"

### Movement Speed Philosophy
- "Movement speed is one of the most strongest stats in the game because it's basically having mobility"
- "Yorick doesn't have dashes and with our E nerf only slowing for 1.5 seconds, being faster does help"
- Celerity + MS shard = base 352 MS, matching counters (Trundle, Nasus, Jax, Tryndamere at 349)

### Phase Rush vs Jax Specific
[17:01-18:38]
- Wall him as he jumps
- Proc Phase Rush, kite away
- When wall is down and he tries to hit you, kite away so he can't auto
- "Jax only wins because of his counter strike. So once it's down, you can just fight him for free"
- Use MS from Phase Rush to stay in and out of fight
- Ghost to walk him down when Q is up and he's stuck

### Phase Rush vs Gwen Specific
[31:07-31:48]
- Phase Rush gives slow resistance vs her ult/Rylai's
- Use MS to survive counter matchup or chase when she makes mistakes
- "By using Phase Rush I actually managed to just survive the matchup or beat her many times"

### Macro/Splitpush with Phase Rush
- Release Maiden in side lanes for pressure
- Group with team temporarily while Maiden pushes
- Wall people for team, debuff with BC + E
- "Your job is to pressure, stall, set up, and recon (warding)"
- Use Phase Rush to go in/out during teamfights without dying
- Split damage between towers — Bastion Breaker one, Demolish the other

### Item Mentions
- Iceborn Cleaver + Bastion Breaker combo
- Dead Man's Plate for armor + MS + tower damage
- Hollow Radiance for MR + pet waveclear
- Black Cleaver for armor shred (ghouls apply it)
- "Tanky splitpush build" = DMP + Hollow Radiance

### Difficulty Scale Mentioned
[36:16] "easy, medium, advanced, hard, and extreme" — confirms the 5-tier system
